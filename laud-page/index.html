Kazi Nazrul Islam

The National Poet of Bangladesh

Rebel Post

Kazi Nazrul Islam is the National poet of Bangladesh who is especially recognised as a Bidrohi kobi(Rebel Post) around the country. Nazrul is a famous poet, musician and philosopher of Bangladesh. The famous poet Nazrul's writings deals with the revolutio of that period os the British government. His revolutionary attempt him to be a National poet of Bangladesh. He has raised his voice against any kind of fascism and oppression and got popularity swiftly around the Indian sub-continent.

Awesome Writer

Nazrul's writings explored themes such as love, freedom, humanity and revolution. He opposed all forms of bigotryan and fundementalism,including religious, caste-based and gender-based. Throughout his career, Nazrul's wrote short stories, novels, and essays but best known for his song and poems. He pioneered new music forms such as Bengali ghazals, Nazul wrote and composed music for nearly 4000 songs (many recordedon HMV and gromophone records)collectively known as Nazul Geeti.

"Love has no meaning or amount."-Kazi Nazrul Islam
